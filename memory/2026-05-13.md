## Video Model Research Handoff

- Did a quick current scan for local NSFW short video generation. The practical
  default community direction looked like Wan, especially Wan 2.1 / 2.2 I2V
  14B, usually through ComfyUI.
- For this box and use case, the recommended starting path was:
  `Wan 2.1 I2V 14B 480p FP8` plus `ComfyUI-WanVideoWrapper`, with
  `ComfyUI-VideoHelperSuite` and possibly KJNodes depending on the workflow.
- The recommended workflow shape was image-first rather than pure text-to-video:
  generate/control a strong first frame with the existing image stack, then use
  Wan I2V to animate a few seconds of simple repetitive motion, optionally
  adding a purpose-trained Wan motion/action LoRA.
- Wan 2.1 was called out as a safer first target than chasing every newer
  variant because it has a mature local workflow and LoRA ecosystem.
